Places Must Visit

Universal Studios

I had visited in 2018

Universal studios is a great place to visit. It is very fun and has lots of rides. You can also get good food, drinks, and sweets.

Timezone

I had visited in 2016

Timezone was a very fun place. I got to play games, get prizes and get sweets.

Three Samurai

I had visited many times this year and last.

Three Samurai is a very good place to get sushi etc. in Iowa. I always enjoy ggoing there. My favorite thing to get is miso soup and shrimp tempara rolls.
